Ten years in the making 💫

From a tiny bundle in 2016 to the bright, record-breaking ten-year-old he is today — here's a look back at how Reyansh grew, one birthday at a time, cheered on every single day by the family's “Apna Group”.

2017 · Turning 1

The littlest star ⭐

One year old — and already the heart of every gathering, in his favourite green-and-yellow.

“Reyansh ko to godi mein hi rakhna padega” — still small enough to be carried everywhere.
2018 · Turning 2

Two & terrific 🎈

Suspenders, a bow-tie, and arms thrown wide open to the world. The birthday treat? A trip to watch Incredibles 2 on his very birthday.

  • His first mundan ceremony.
  • Papa's nickname for him: “R²” (R-squared 🤓).
  • Famously nibbled the little packet from a shoe box 😅.
2019 · Turning 3

Cheeky & three 😜

All silly grins and partner-in-crime energy with the cousins — and already a little bookworm.

  • Qualified for an inter-school competition.
  • A fresh stack of books — and his first science test.
2020 · Turning 4

The big home birthday 🏠

The lockdown year — so the family brought the party home, with a cake that proudly read “Reyansh” and cake-cutting over a video call.

Mummy: “Why do you write all your numbers backwards?”  Reyansh: “But I don't write 1 backwards!” 😄
2021 · Turning 5

High five! ✋

Superhero spirit, a costume to match, and his name spelled out in gold balloons.

  • Won the carrom final — against a 9-year-old, at just five!
  • Built a Pac-Man game in Scratch with Papa.
2022 · Turning 6

Six & full of energy 🎉

Bigger smiles, bigger balloons — and a birthday boy now sporting a karate yellow belt.

  • Ran his first 5K — people stopped to take photos with him!
  • School Rank 5 in the IGKO Olympiad.
  • Learning chess moves (and the queen-bishop trick 😏).
2023 · Turning 7

A record-breaking year 👑

A grand party fit for the birthday boy — and a year of seriously big achievements.

  • 🏆 National Record (India Book of Records) — named 68 countries' flags in one minute… while hula-hooping!
  • Started learning Python coding.
2024 · Turning 8
🧩

Sharp as ever

  • Came first in the kids' Sudoku championship.
  • Climbing the ranks in the Maths Olympiad.
  • Onto the cricket pitch — match after match.
2025 · Turning 9
🏃‍♂️

Always on the move

  • Finished the Mumbai Junior Miler 2-mile run in 24:20.
  • Ran his own stall at the school fair with friends.
  • Leading everyone in Surya Namaskar & learning Sanskrit shlokas.
2026 · Turning 10 🎂
🏏

Double digits!

  • Hitting sixes in every match and winning games with the Sunday cricket group.
  • Standard 5, and growing up so fast — “Reyansh toh bahut bada lag raha hai”.
“Reyansh kitna cute tha pehle” — and look how big you've grown. ❤️
